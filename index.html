An open forum for developers and users
to interact with each other

























Bug Support is a simple to use, open forum for basic and advanced users to interact with developers of mobile apps. Users can login to this service and send feedback right to the developer of the application. The current process for developers to get feedback from their users, is through the App Store or Google Play, and leaving a review on their application. Most reviews give the app a single star because there might be a small bug. This site will help developers to keep their reviews clean and their bugs seperate from the review.
